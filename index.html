About NeurClaws
NeuroPaws is dedicated to enhancing the bond between pets and their owners by providing insight into pets’ emotional and mental well-being.Our wearable technology measures stress, cognitive function and more to help you understand your pet’s state of mind.
Key Features
Stress monitoring
Track your pet’s stress levels and get real-time insights into their emotional state.
Cognitive insights
Analyze your pet’s cognitive function and behaviour patterns to keep them mentally health.
Bluetooth connectivity
Sync data seamlessly to your mobile device and access insight anytime, anywhere.
